What is Orkestrim ?

A National Level Technical Symposium

The department of Electronics and Instrumentation of Valliammai Engineering college has such a day. 25thaugust, 2018, gets anointed with orkestrim’18 . Machines and instruments are defined the way as a man needs them to be. But in the case of men, knowledge is what defines them. Showcase your mind and your ideas through the presentations and the challenges at various events held on the day and win the honour and exciting prizes you’ve ever wanted for it. But that’s not all of it , Behold for the day where instrumentation join hands with green collar work to strive together for a better tomorrow embracing the new field of GREEN COLLAR INSTRUMENTATION. Register now and welcome aboard, orkestrim’18 is coming…
